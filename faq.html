Shipping & Delivery

What are your delivery options and timeframes?

We offer several delivery options to fit your needs. Standard delivery arrives in 5-7 business days and includes curbside drop-off. Our White Glove delivery service includes room-of-choice placement and professional assembly, typically scheduled within 2-3 weeks depending on your location. For larger items like sofas and beds, White Glove is often required. Delivery is free on orders over $999.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! Our White Glove delivery service includes professional assembly at no extra charge. Our trained technicians will assemble your furniture, place it in your desired location, and remove all packaging materials. For items already delivered via standard shipping, you can schedule standalone assembly service for a flat fee of $99 per item. Assembly appointments are typically available within 3-5 business days.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window on most items, giving you time to ensure your furniture is the perfect fit. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and tags intact. Custom orders, clearance items, and mattresses have modified return policies due to their personalized nature. Return shipping is free for defective items; a $99 pickup fee applies for other returns. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

What is your warranty policy?

All furniture comes with a minimum 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Many items include extended warranties: solid wood furniture has a 5-year warranty, upholstered pieces have a 3-year warranty on frames and cushions, and outdoor furniture includes a 2-year weather resistance guarantee. Warranties do not cover normal wear and tear, damage from misuse, or issues resulting from improper assembly. Extended protection plans are available at checkout for comprehensive coverage.

Products & Availability

How do I check if an item is in stock?

Real-time availability is displayed on each product page, showing current stock levels and estimated delivery windows. If an item is temporarily out of stock, you can sign up for email notifications to be alerted when it becomes available again. Many items also show estimated restock dates so you can plan accordingly. Our AI Home Advisor can also check availability across our entire catalog instantly.

Can I order fabric swatches before purchasing?

Absolutely! We offer free fabric swatches for any upholstered furniture so you can see and feel the material in your own space. Order up to 5 swatches at a time with free standard shipping. Swatches typically arrive within 3-5 business days. Each swatch includes care instructions and fabric composition details. This helps ensure the color and texture match your existing decor before committing to a purchase.

Design Services

Do you offer interior design consultations?

Yes, we provide complimentary design consultations to help you create your perfect space! Choose from virtual consultations via video call, in-store appointments with our design specialists, or submit photos for personalized room planning recommendations. Our designers can help with everything from selecting individual pieces to planning entire room layouts. Sessions typically last 30-45 minutes and include a personalized shopping list tailored to your style and budget.

Can I visualize furniture in my space before buying?

Our AR Room Visualizer lets you see furniture in your actual space using your smartphone camera. Simply point your phone where you want to place the item, and you'll see a true-to-scale 3D model. You can also use our digital Mood Board tool to create custom room layouts, save your favorite pieces, and share designs with family or friends. Both tools are free and available on our website and mobile app.

Orders & Payment

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also view real-time order status in your account dashboard, including detailed delivery window estimates and driver location on delivery day. For White Glove deliveries, you'll receive a call 24 hours before to confirm your delivery window. Our AI Home Advisor can also provide instant order status updates anytime.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for your convenience. Financing is available through Affirm with 0% APR for 12 months on purchases over $500, subject to credit approval. Business accounts can apply for net-30 payment terms. Gift cards are also available and can be combined with other payment methods.
